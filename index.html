About Me

Who am I?

I am a passionate and detail-oriented Frontend Developer with a BSc in Management Information Systems (Second Class Lower Division, GPA: 3.24).

With over two years of professional experience at Insfra Technologies, I’m a passionate web and mobile app developer with a strong focus on creating clean, user-friendly interfaces. I enjoy bringing ideas to life — from designing UI in Figma to building responsive applications that perform seamlessly across all devices.

My skill set includes front-end and back-end development, UI/UX design, and proficiency with tools like Figma, Canva, Photoshop, and the full Microsoft Office suite. I’m also experienced in WordPress development, including custom theme and plugin development, which allows me to build tailored solutions that meet both design and functionality requirements.

Whether I’m working on a simple landing page or a full-scale mobile application, I pay close attention to detail and always aim for high-quality results. I’m a fast learner, hardworking, and always eager to take on new challenges. Driven by creativity, I take pride in delivering smart solutions that exceed expectations.
